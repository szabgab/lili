Introduction

Plurals

In this exercise you will learn some simple plural forms of German.
Most German nouns end with one of these forms:
* „n/en“ * „r/er“ * „e“ * „s“ have either no special ending or no plural at all.
Tip: In German all nouns use the article "die" in the plural!
