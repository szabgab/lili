# ser In Ladino there are two words for "to be": ser and estar * yo so/se - I am * tu sos - you are * el es - he is * eya es - she is * mozotros somos/semos - we are * mozotras somos/semos - we are * vozotros sosh - you are (plural masc.) * vozotras sosh - you are (plural fem.) * eyos son - they are (masc.) * eyas son - they are (fem.)
