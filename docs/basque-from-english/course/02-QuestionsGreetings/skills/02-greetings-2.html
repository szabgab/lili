Vocabulary Greetngs 2

  • Agur: Good-bye
  • Bihar arte: See you tomorrow(lit: tomorrow until)
  • Ez da ezer: It's nothing
  • Gabon: Good Night (after 20:00 or when go to bed)
  • Gero arte: See you later (lit: later until)
  • Berandu: Late
  • Ongi etorri: Welcome
