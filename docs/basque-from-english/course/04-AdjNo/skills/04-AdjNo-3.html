Introduction

â€‹Adjectives & No 3

  • Basque has two words for short, Labur and Motz. Labur is short in length and Motz is short in height.

Vocabulary Adjectives & No 3

  • handi: big
  • txiki:small
  • altu: tall
  • labur: short (length)
  • motz: short (height)
  • gizena: fat
  • argal: thin
  • gazte: young
  • zahar: old
  • morea: purple
