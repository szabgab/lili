Introduction

## Possessive adjectives * Note that possessive adjectives, unlike normal adjectives, go before the noun and do not change when the noun is plural. * Hura nire laguan da.: He is my friend. * Haiek nire lagunak dira. : They are my friends. ### Culture note * For the Basque, the home and relatives do not belong to just one person, so they would not say my house/father/etc. but * Gure etxea/aita/ama/aitonia/amona (our house/father/mother/grandfather/grandmother). ### Vocabulary This &amp; To Whom 2 * nire: my * zure: your * bere: his/hers/its * gure: our * zuen: your (plural) * haien: their [ Go Back to Home Page!](..)
