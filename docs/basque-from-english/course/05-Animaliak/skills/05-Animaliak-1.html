Basque names of common animals

  • The Basque region is largely agriculture, so you are going to see animals.

Vocabulary Animaliak 1

  • animalia: animal
  • hontza: a/the owl
  • katu: cat
  • oilasko: chicken
  • txakur: dog
  • txoria: bird
